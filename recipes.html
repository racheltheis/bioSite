Jeff's Favorite Ice Cream Recipes

Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

View Original Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups heavy cream
  • 1½ cups whole milk
  • ⅔ cup cane sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine cream, milk, sugar, vanilla, and salt.
  2. Warm over medium-low heat, whisking often, for 5 minutes until sugar dissolves.
  3. Chill mixture for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Churn in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer’s instructions.
  5. Freeze for 2 to 4 hours for firmer texture.

Rich Chocolate Ice Cream

View Original Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 oz dark chocolate, chopped
  • 2 oz milk chocolate, chopped
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 tbsp Dutch-process cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolates over a double boiler.
  2. Heat milk, cream, sugar, condensed milk, and cocoa powder until smooth.
  3. Combine chocolate with hot dairy mixture.
  4. Chill the mixture for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Churn in an ice cream maker and freeze until firm.
